How to play Dangling-Boy:

Each player takes a turn guessing a random word letter by letter. You are given 5 wrong guesses before you fail the round.

If you guess the word correctly then you are awarded 100 points. If you guess wrong then you recieve 0 points. After your turn the next player is up

The game is played to 1000 points. The first person to get there wins.

Player 1 goes first!
